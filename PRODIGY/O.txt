X is a rhythm game inspired from titles such as 'Taiko no Tatsujin', 'Beatmania IIDX', 'Elite Beat Agents', 'O2Jam', 'StepMania' and 'DJMax'. X has all its levels made by its community through the in-game editor. As of March 2022, X had more than 19 million monthly active users. X is played for fun, to supposedly decreasing your reaction time, vibe with all the various songs and probably get Tendonitis or the Carpal Tunnel Syndrome.

X is also seen being played often by a very famous professional VALORANT player.
