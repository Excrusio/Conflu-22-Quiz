In Japanese mythology, X & Y are both husband & wife and brother & sister. They were tasked by the elder gods to create the physical world and hence eventually gave birth to the land of Japan and a host of other gods. However, when giving birth to the fire god Kagutsuchi, was severely burned and eventually died. In anger and grief, X killed Kagutsuchi and went to the underworld looking for Y but was unsuccessful in bringing her back and instead brought back death to the world. To purify himself, he washed his face from which, 3 new deities were born: A (sun goddess), from his left eye, B (moon god), from his right eye and C (storm god), from his nose.
