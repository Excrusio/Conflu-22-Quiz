The Pokemon Drowzee is inspired from a real life herbivorous mammal X native to the forests of South and Central America. X is similar in shape to a pig but is related to horses and rhinoceroses. Drowzee's signature move, Y is also inspired from a famous myth which X is supposed to do and is feared by many people. The myth involves Baku, Japanese supernatural beings said to devour nightmares. ID X & Y.
