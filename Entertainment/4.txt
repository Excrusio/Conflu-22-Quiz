X is a 1993 biographical crime drama film based on the true story of the Guildford Four, four people falsely convicted of the 1974 IRA Guildford pub bombings, which killed four off-duty British soldiers and a civilian. The film focuses on Gerard Conlon (portrayed by Daniel Day Lewis nominated for Academy Awards' Best Actor in Leading Role) who spends the next 15 years trying to prove his innocence.
