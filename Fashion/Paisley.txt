The word X derives from the English town of same name in west Scotland which was famous for the production of  a textile pattern of the same name. X is a type of pattern having a curved teardrop shape known as Y which is Persian for 'flower'. X having Indian or Persian origin became famous in the form of luxurious Kashmiri Shawls which were used as a kingly gift in 16th Century Persia & for European Officers. It also became famous partly by the Beatles since it had a certain psychedelic style and exoticism.
