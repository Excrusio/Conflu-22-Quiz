The word X derives from the English town of same name in the west Scotland which was famous for the production of  a textile pattern of the same name X. X is a type of pattern having a curved teardrop shape known as Y which is Persian for 'flower'. X having Indian or Persian origin became famous in the form of luxurious Kashmiri Shawls which were used a kingly gift in 16th Century Persia & European Officers. It also became famous partly by the Beatles since it had a certain psychedelic style and exoticism.
