X-ism refers to the idea that all human beings are members of a single community whose adherents are known as X. The word X derives from Ancient Greek meaning 'citizens of the world' and is also used as a word for a cocktail made with Vodka, Cranberry Juice, Triple Sec and a hint of lime which is quite frequently mentioned in Sex and the City. X is also a famous American fashion and entertainment best-selling magazine for women.
