The X principle is the practice in T.V. series or movies to include one stereotypical female character in an all male ensemble. It establishes a male-dominated narrative, where the woman is the exception and exists only in reference to the men. Some examples of such media are: Ocean's Eleven, Trinity - The Matrix, Leia - Star Wars, Penny - The Big Bang Theory, April - TNMT and of course, ______, the Phyrgian-cap wearing comic character after whom the principle is named after.
