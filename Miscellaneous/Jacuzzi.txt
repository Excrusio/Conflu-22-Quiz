A family of seven brothers and six sisters emigrated to California in early 1920s and were involved in inventing and designing aviation and water pumps in the field of aviation and agriculture. They achieved notable feats in the industry by creating a very successful submersible pump. When a young member of the family was diagnosed with rheumatoid arthritis, the pump was used to ease his pain and was remade into a portable model called J-300 which launched them into the wellness industry.

Identify the family which have the company of the same name.
